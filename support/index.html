Privacy Policy for Pomodoro Pro

Pomodoro Pro Privacy Statement

Pomodoro Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. All data used by the application is processed locally on your device and remains under your control.

The app does not transmit personally identifiable information to external servers, nor does it require an account to function. Any settings, timers, or local usage information remain on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share them.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, we will post the updated policy here with an updated effective date.
